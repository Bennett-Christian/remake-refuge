Originals VS Remakes

The most successful video games often get remade in order for modern audiences to get a chance at experiencing what they had to offer. This site aims to help people explore more about these remakes from an outside perspective.

Some remakes are considered to be better than their originals, while others end up being poorly received. There are multiple different factors that go into producing a well-liked remake, but ultimately, I think fans hope for remakes to deliver the same familiar feelings that caused them to fall in love with the originals in the first place.

With these things in mind, please feel free to browse around the different remakes showcased on this site. You may just discover a new game worth checking out!
