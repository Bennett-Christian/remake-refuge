Credits

The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening

Released in 1993 for the Game Boy, developed by Nintendo. Remade in 2019 for the Nintendo Switch by Grezzo.

Final Fantasy VII

Released in 1997 for the Playstation, developed by Square. Remade in 2020 for the Playstation 5.

Secret of Mana

Released in 1993 for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System, developed by Square. Remade in 2018 for the Playstation 4.

Demon's Souls

Released in 2009 for the Playstation 3, developed by FromSoftware. Remade in 2020 for the Playstation 5.

Shadow of the Colossus

Released in 2005 for the Playstation 2, developed by Japan Studio and Team Ico. Remade in 2011 for the Playstation 3. Remade again in 2018 for the Playstation 4.
